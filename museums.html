Museums

1. Albert Hall Museum
The building gets its name from The Victoria and Albert Museum in London, the inspiration for its design

2. Ahar Museum
Ahar Museum is in close proximity to an impressive cluster of cenotaphs of the Maharanas of Mewar. The museum has a small, but rare collection of earthen pottery.

3.Ajmer govt. museum
The museum is housed within the magnificent fortified palace of the legendary Mughal Emperor Akbar, which was built in 1570.

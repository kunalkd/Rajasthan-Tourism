State Ministry

Ministry Name:Vasundhara Raje Scindia

Ministry Address : State Hotel, Khasa Kothi, Jaipur, 302001, Rajasthan

Capital: Jaipur

Spoken Languages: Rajasthani, Hindi

Largest Cities: Jaipur,Jodhpur,Kota, Bikaner,Ajmer

It is a state of men with proud mustaches and women with twinkling anklets in colorful swirling ghagras. The landscape is dotted with island palaces shimmering on idyllic blue lakes; temples and fortresses situated on hilltops of the rugged and rocky Aravalli; exquisite palaces built during the reign of some of its many erstwhile Rajput dynasties; and well laid out gardens with pavilions and kiosks, all of which add to the splendor and whimsical charm of this majestic land. However, Rajasthan is not only fort, palaces, and culture. The state is also one place in India, which has marched ahead with its development programs. Problems of the people have remained as the central idea and governance has been put in the hands of the villagers itself. Surely, this state is one destination in India, from where no one can return empty handed.

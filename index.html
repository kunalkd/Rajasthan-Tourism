Incredible Rajasthan


"Padhaaro Mhaare Des..."

1. Amber fort
Amer, originally, was the capital of the state before Jaipur. It is a fort, built in 1592 by Raja Man Singh.

2. City palace,Udaipur
City Palace, Udaipur, is a palace located on the eastern banks of Pichola lake, Rajasthan.

3.Mehrangarh fort
Mehrangarh located in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, is one of the largest forts in India.

4. Kumbhalgarh
Kumbhalgarh is a Mewar fortress on the westerly range of Aravalli Hills.

5. Ranakpur
Ranakpur is widely known for its marble Jain temple. It is located between Jodhpur and Udaipur.

6. Pushkar
Pushkar Lake, a sacred Hindu site with 52 ghats, where pilgrims bathe.

7. Ranthambore
Ranthambore National Park is a vast wildlife reserve near the town of Sawai Madhopur in Rajasthan.

8. Thar desert
The Thar Desert, also known as the Great Indian Desert,it covers an area of 200,000 km².
